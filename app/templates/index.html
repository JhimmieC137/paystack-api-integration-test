About this application


This is webapp was created to demonstrate the intergration of at least two of Paystack's API's

  • It demonstrates three of their payment API's and one of their miscellaneous API's (Bank List)
  • These API's are integrated and are functioning on test Secret and Public Keys
  • Hence even the Pop up intergration is a test case and will not perform actual payment

Each of these API's are crucial and helpful for any payment platform

List Bank API


This Api gets a list of all supported banks within a specified country of paystack's coverage, and their properties.
To see a demonstration, type in a country e.g Nigeria
